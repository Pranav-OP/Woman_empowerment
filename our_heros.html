Pillars of Change



Mother Teresa

Mother Teresa was born Agnes Gonxha Bojaxhiu in Skopje*, Macedonia, on August 26**, 1910.

















Indira Gandhi

Indira Gandhi (born November 19, 1917, Allahabad, India—died October 31, 1984, New Delhi), politician who served as prime minister of India.

















Kalpana Chawla

Kalpana Chawla (March 17, 1962 – February 1, 2003) was an American astronaut, engineer, and the first woman of Indian descent to go to space.

















Indra Nooyi

Indra Nooyi, (born October 28, 1955, Madras [now Chennai], India), Indian-born American businesswoman.

















Ela Bhatt

Ela Bhatt (born September 7, 1933, Ahmedabad, India), founder of the Self-Employed Women’s Association (SEWA).

















Mary Kom

Mangte Chungneijang Mary Kom (born 1 March 1983) is an Indian Olympic boxer and incumbent Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha.

















Dipa Karmakar

Dipa Karmakar (born 9 August 1993) is an Indian artistic gymnast.

















Elizabeth Garret Anderson

Elizabeth (9 June 1836 – 17 December 1917). She was the first woman to qualify in Britain as a physician and surgeon.

















Tania Sachdev

Tania Sachdev (born 20 August 1986) is an Indian chess player, who holds the titles of International Master (IM) and Woman Grandmaster (WGM).

















Kirthiga Reddy

Kirthiga Reddy (born c.1971).She is former managing director of Facebook India.
